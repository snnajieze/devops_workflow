About Me

I'm a Software Engineer specializing in AI Automation, Cloud Engineering, and DevOps, with 7+ years of experience building, deploying, and scaling production-grade systems. My background in PHP/Laravel evolved into full ownership of cloud infrastructure, automation workflows, and system reliability.
